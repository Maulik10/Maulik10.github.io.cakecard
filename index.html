🍫 Chocolate Cake

Prep Time: 45 mins

  • 1 ½ cups flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • ½ cup cocoa powder
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 1 cup water
  • ½ cup oil
  • 1 tbsp vinegar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  1. Preheat oven to 350°F.
  2. Mix dry ingredients.
  3. Add wet ingredients and stir.
  4. Pour into greased pan.
  5. Bake for 30–35 minutes.
  6. Cool and enjoy!
